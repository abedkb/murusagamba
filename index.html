Our Products

Agrometeorological Services & Products

From daily field observations to seasonal forecasts — decision support for Tanzania’s agricultural sector

Daily Observations
Meteorological Observations
Structured daily weather data collection at 0600 and 1200 UTC across all agrometeorological stations.
  • Temperature: dry/wet bulb, max, min, dew point
  • Rainfall, wind run, sunshine hours, radiation
  • Soil temperature and evaporation pan data
  • WhatsApp / SMS sharing with one click
Seasonal Forecast
Seasonal Weather Forecasts
Spatial rainfall outlooks for the OND and MAM seasons — enabling early planning across Tanzania’s agroclimatic zones.
  • Zone-based rainfall probability maps
  • Above / Near / Below normal rainfall outlook
  • Temperature and onset/cessation guidance
  • Regional and district-level advisories
Agrometeorological Bulletin
Crop Weather Advisories
Periodic agrometeorological bulletins informing farmers, extension officers and agricultural planners across Tanzania.
  • Crop calendar and planting advisories
  • Plant phenology monitoring and reports
  • Rainfall distribution and crop stress analysis
  • Extreme weather impact assessment
